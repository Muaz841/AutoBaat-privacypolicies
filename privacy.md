# Privacy Policy for AutoBaat

Last updated: March 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how AutoBaat ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects data when you use our service. We comply with applicable laws, including the Pakistan Personal Data Protection Bill (PDPB) and GDPR equivalents.

## 1. What data we collect
When a business connects their Instagram or WhatsApp account to our platform via Meta APIs, we receive and process:
- Messages sent by their customers (text, images, media)
- Customer identifiers (phone numbers, usernames)
- Business account information (only as needed for automation)
**We do not collect data from children under 13, and our service is not directed at children.**

## 2. How we use the data and purposes of processing
We use the data for the following purposes (based on consent from the business owner and legitimate interest in providing the service):
- To provide messaging automation (auto-replies, order detection)
- To maintain conversation context so replies make sense
- To improve our AI (messages are stored anonymously for future model training)
**We process data only as necessary for the service and do not use it for advertising or unrelated purposes.**

## 3. Data sharing
We do **not** sell, rent, or share your data with third parties, except:
- With Meta APIs (required for Instagram/WhatsApp integration)
- As legally required (e.g., court orders)
- With secure service providers (e.g., cloud storage) under strict data protection agreements

## 4. Data storage & security
- Messages are stored in our secure database
- Access tokens are encrypted
- We use HTTPS everywhere
- Data is kept only while the account is connected + 90 days after disconnection (unless you request deletion earlier)
We implement industry-standard security measures to protect against unauthorized access, loss, or alteration.

## 5. Your rights
You (the business owner) can:
- Access your stored data
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of all stored messages/data at any time (email us; we process within 30 days)
- Disconnect your accounts in the dashboard
- Contact us for any questions or complaints
